"Life, Liberty, and the pursuit of Happiness" – no other city quite captures the American dream so well. Full of anticipation, it’s a place that reveals something new and unexpected around every turn. New York City is constantly reinventing itself. Tribeca has gone from downtown-dive to super-chic in the space of a lifetime, while areas like Chelsea and the East Village continue to be popular. Manhattan’s grid-like layout makes it easy to navigate. Enjoy an NYC-style bagel and morning stroll around Central Park, followed by an afternoon exploring the Met and Guggenheim Museums. Walk hand-in-hand with a loved one across the iconic Brooklyn Bridge before diving head first into the city’s legendary nightlife.
